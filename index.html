About this site

This site makes extensive use of Tailwind, CSS, JavaScript, and LocalStorage. If you press the 'start button', the working time lasts for 25 minutes, then it takes a 5-minute break and saves it to memory. If you click the 'clear button', it clears and starts the time from the beginning. You can also pause the time. And also shows the time in 'title'
